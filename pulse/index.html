FAQ

Quick answers

Is PULSE really free?

Completely. No ads, no in-app purchases, no real-money anything. It's a pure skill game.

Do I need an account?

No. Pick a tag when you post a score. No sign-up and no personal data collected.

How do you stop cheaters?

Your gameplay input log is replayed server-side through the exact same simulation code. The board stores the score the server computes, so a forged number never gets in.

Where can I play?

Any browser on phone or desktop, right now. The iOS app is on the way to the App Store.

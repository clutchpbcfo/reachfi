06 / Questions

Frequently asked.

What does "owned network" actually mean? +
It means we operate these four accounts directly. When you book a campaign, we're not calling around to negotiate with influencers — we publish to channels we control, on our schedule. That's why we can guarantee placement and hand you real analytics.
Why is that better than a normal KOL agency? +
Three reasons: price (no middleman markup), speed (no negotiation lag), and trust (first-party analytics you can verify, not screenshots). A broker rents you reach and disappears. We own the reach, so we're accountable for it.
What kind of reach can I actually expect? +
The network spans 1M+ followers across four channels, from a 668K-follower flagship to focused trading and NFT audiences. Exact reach depends on which channels your campaign uses; we scope projected numbers in the channel-map step and report actuals after.
Do you guarantee price action? +
No — and anyone who does is lying or about to get sued. We guarantee reach, placement, and verified analytics. What the market does with the attention is up to your project.
Which channel is right for my project? +
Depends on your goal. Need broad awareness? The big culture channels. Need to reach people who actually trade and ape? The DeFi and trading channels. Most campaigns blend both — mass reach to get seen, DeFi reach to get bought. We map it in step two.
How do I book? +
DM us. Tell us your project and timeline, and we'll come back with a channel map and a quote. Time-sensitive launch? Say so — we can move fast when the slot's open.

03 / How It Runs

Four steps. No drift.

Commissions run like broadcasts: scheduled, scoped, and shipped. Here is the whole process.

STEP 01 · SCOPE

Bring the idea

Any shape is fine: a paragraph, a deck, a dead website. We answer with a fixed number and a real timeline, including the store gates nobody warns you about, like Google's mandatory closed-test window.

STEP 02 · PREPAID

Payment lands first

The quote is paid in full before the build starts. SOL, USDC, or invoice. First paid, first built: studio slots open between network releases.

STEP 03 · BUILD

Days, not months

Your commission runs on the same pipeline that ships our own titles, so the tooling is already warm. You see the work as it lands, not at a big reveal.

STEP 04 · HANDOVER

You own the keys

Your store accounts, your signing keys, your repo, your analytics. All of it, from day one. Updates after handover are quoted separately, the same way.

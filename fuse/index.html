The Game

Greed is the mechanic

Most timing games give you a safe zone and ask you to hit it. FUSE gives you a burning fuse and a choice. Every millisecond you wait is worth more, right up until the bomb makes it worth nothing.

GREED PAYS

No target zone. The value climbs as the spark gets closer to the bomb. The biggest cuts happen one frame before disaster.

ONE BLADE, NO MERCY

Tap a lane to cut its fuse and bank the points. Multiple lanes burn at once, each at its own speed. Pick your order and keep your nerve.

THE BOMB DECIDES

Hold a lane too long and it detonates. The run is over, no appeals. The bomb ends the argument.

FAQ

Quick answers

Is FUSE really free?

Completely. No ads, no in-app purchases, no real-money anything. The bombs are cartoon arcade objects in a timing game.

Do I need an account?

No. Pick a tag when you post a score. No sign-up and no personal data collected.

How do you stop cheaters?

Your tap timeline is replayed server-side through the same simulation the game runs locally. The board stores the score from the replay, never the number a client claims.

Where can I play?

Any browser on phone or desktop, right now. The iOS app is on the way to the App Store.

FAQ

Quick answers

Is FUSE really free?

Completely. No ads, no in-app purchases, no real-money anything. The bombs are cartoon arcade objects and the only thing at stake is your pride.

Do I need an account?

No. Pick a tag when you post a score. No sign-up, no personal data collected.

How do you stop cheaters?

Your tap timeline is replayed server-side through the same deterministic simulation the game runs locally. The board stores the replay's score — a forged number can never pass.

Where can I play?

Any browser, phone or desktop, right now. The iOS app is in App Review and lands on the App Store imminently. 1v1 duels arrive with the arcade's PvP rollout.

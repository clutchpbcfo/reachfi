Privacy Policy — INSERT COIN

Last updated: June 30, 2026

INSERT COIN ("the app", "we") is a skill arcade from Reach Fi containing original single-player games. We built it to be fun and respectful of your privacy. This policy explains what we store and why.

What we collect

  • An anonymous player tag — only if you choose to enter one for a leaderboard. Use any name you like; it does not need to be your real name.
  • Gameplay data — your score and the input log of a run. The input log is sent with your score so our server can replay the run and verify the score is genuine. We also keep aggregate play counts to understand how the games are used.
  • Game Center — if you are signed in to Game Center, leaderboard rankings are handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. We receive only the ranking data needed to show the boards.

What we do NOT collect

  • No real names, emails, phone numbers, or accounts are required to play.
  • No precise location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data with third parties. No ads, no tracking.

How data is stored

Leaderboard entries are stored on our hosting and database providers solely to operate the games. Local preferences and progress are kept in your device storage.

Children

INSERT COIN does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no account or personal data is required, the games can be enjoyed without sharing identifying information.

Your choices

You can clear your local data at any time in your device settings, which resets your local player data. To request removal of a leaderboard entry, contact us below.

Frequently asked

How do leaderboards and scores work?

Scores post to Game Center under a tag you choose. Every run is verified on our server by replaying your inputs, so the boards stay honest. No account or sign-up is required to play.

My score didn't post to the leaderboard.

Leaderboards need a connection, and Game Center must be signed in on your device (Settings → Game Center). The games play fully offline; scores sync once you're back online. If a score still doesn't appear, email us the game and approximate time and we'll look into it.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no login and no personal data required. You pick a display tag for the boards, and that's it.

Is there a daily challenge?

Yes — each game has a daily seed that's identical for everyone that day, so the daily boards are a level field. Come back tomorrow for a new one.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email clutchpbcfo@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version. Screenshots or a screen recording help a lot.
